Panakam

Description

Panakam is a traditional Telugu summer drink made with jaggery, lemon, black pepper, and cardamom. It is usually prepared during Sri Rama Navami and other festive occasions, but it also makes a great natural energy drink on hot days.

Sweet, tangy, and lightly spiced, Panakam is a refreshing and healthy beverage that cools the body and satisfies thirst.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup grated jaggery
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry ginger powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve the grated jaggery in water and strain to remove any impurities.
  2. Add lemon juice, cardamom powder, black pepper powder, dry ginger powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Stir well to combine all the flavors.
  4. Chill or serve at room temperature.
