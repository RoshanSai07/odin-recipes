Biryani

Description

Biryani is a fragrant, flavorful rice dish layered with tender meat, aromatic spices, and herbs. It is one of the most beloved dishes across South Asia, known for its rich taste and beautiful presentation.

This recipe combines basmati rice with marinated chicken and traditional spices, cooked together to perfection in a single pot. The result is a mouth-watering, satisfying meal perfect for gatherings or family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt, to taste
  • 3 cups water
  • Cooking oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot, add the whole spices, then fry the onions until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is nearly done.
  6. Add coriander and mint leaves, then layer the drained basmati rice over the chicken.
  7. Pour in water, sprinkle garam masala, cover tightly, and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  8. Gently fluff the biryani, garnish with more coriander, and serve hot.
